About Goldenspoon

Where Taste Meets Celebration

Founded in 2021, Golden Spoon is dedicated to delivering memorable catering experiences for every occasion. The company is led by MOHD Kamran, who brings over 20 years of experience in the hospitality industry, including 6 years of specialized catering expertise.

From intimate gatherings to grand celebrations, we create carefully crafted menus that combine rich flavors, quality ingredients, and elegant presentation to make every event special. Our focus on taste, service, and hospitality has helped Golden Spoon become a trusted choice for premium catering.
